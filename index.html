Frequently Asked Questions


1. What documents are required to rent a vehicle?

You need a valid driver's license, AADHAR card (for Indian residents), and an international driver's license for foreign residents.

2. What is the minimum rental duration?

The minimum rental duration is 1 day.

3. Are there additional charges for late returns?

Yes, late returns are subject to additional charges based on the hourly rental rate.

4. What types of vehicles are available?

We offer cars, bikes, and scooters for rental.

5. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before the start of the rental period. Charges may apply for last-minute cancellations.

6. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and mobile wallets for payment.

7. Is fuel included in the rental price?

Fuel is not included. The vehicle must be returned with the same fuel level as when it was rented.

8. Are there any discounts for long-term rentals?

Yes, discounts are available for rentals exceeding one week. Contact customer service for details.

9. What happens in case of an accident?

You must inform us immediately and file a report. Insurance coverage will depend on the plan you selected.

10. Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental by contacting our support team. Additional charges may apply.
